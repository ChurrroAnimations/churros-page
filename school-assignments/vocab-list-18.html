           Maxwell was, well, a glutton. It seems rude to say, but he wasn't nebulous or contemptible about his eating and drinking habits. His idiosyncrasy is that he likes to eat. He does not consider this trait to be derivative. If someone treats him in a mercurial manner due to his behavior, they are likely to walk away feeling blighted by his wit. When the vegetables burgeon in the summer, Maxwell enjoys walking through the garden and munching. This behavior causes him to have a very odd gait. However, Maxwell swears his doctor will corroborate his claim that he is the epitome of fitness.
