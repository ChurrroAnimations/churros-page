Basic information

Name: Churro

Gender: Unknown
Sexuality: Unknown
Age: 5 years*(See Backstory)

Physical description

Churro is a well-loved plushie. Her fabric a faded deep green, complimented by a lime coloured underbelly dulled by time. Churro's chipped, beady eyes, seated in on either side of her squished dome head. Even whilst devoid of emotion and separate, the pupils convey a story in unison. A field of underbelly hue spanning the two with a derpy V-shaped maw equidistant.

Churro's fur a scratchy and worn fabric. A far cry from her prime, yet a seldom soft lingers, alluding her former. Her stuffing deflated and firm albeit still plump and airy. Churro's nostalgic features providing condolence and comfort just the same.

A being of undecipherable androgyny, in both voice and silhouette. She speaks in soothing and melodic form, uttered in perfect tongue. Churro's voice analogous to that of a young child.

Personality

Churro is sincere, charismatic, and gentle, though vaguely mysterious. A seldom air of unease enshrouds her, hardly detectable. The unrest layered by her ever static face acting an obstacle to intuitively determine her emotions. She has a soft, supple touch, paired with a soft-spoken, almost hypnotic voice. She prefers observe from afar, yet friendly, open and easily manipulated.

Strengths and weaknesses

Churro is a turtle plushie. Thusly susceptible to many forms of damage, such as fire or cuts, exacerbated by her inability in self-repararation. However she is also pliable, squishy, and nimble. Churro's body may also function like a sponge to store scents or young quantities of fluids. Furthermore, she can bear a significant weight of blunt force. Churro is very persuasive and can ease tensions effectively. She can also mimic other objects, shifting into a plush form of it.

Backstory

Churro is a extranatural entity that decided to spontaneously start existing one day, assuming the form of a turtle plushie. Physical description

Sopapilla's body is on the smaller side when compared to other RainWings her age, but quite stout in build. Inversely, her wings are slightly oversized, boasting a considerable wingspan. These features in tandem convey a somewhat comical silhouette.

Sopapilla's scales are a muted pear green with underscales of cerulean blue. Her underbelly is an aged mint green, seeming as though stained with sepia. Her wings are a vibrant fuchsia that fades to a dappled hot pink. Sopapilla's eyes are a dark off-amber yellow color.

Personality

Sopapilla is very bubbly and distant, with her personality changing arbitrarily.

Strengths and weaknesses

In fact, she avoids flying altogether, opting to walk and climb to get around. She also has a rather loss grip on reality, acting erratically. However, while she obscures it, she is really intelligent and able to grasp concepts quickly.
