Sopapilla the RainWing

Basic information

Name: Sopapilla
Species: Dragon (RainWing)
Gender: Cis female (she/her)
Sexuality: Ace
Age: 6.5 dragon years (about 17-18 in human years)

Physical description

Sopapilla's body is on the smaller side when compared to other RainWings her age, but quite stout in build. Inversely, her wings are slightly oversized, boasting a considerable wingspan. These features in tandem convey a somewhat comical silhouette.

Sopapilla's scales are a muted pear green with undertones of cerulean blue. Her underbelly is a somewhat aged mint green, looking almost as though stained with sepia. Her wings are a vibrant fuchsia that fades to a dappled hot pink. Sopapilla's eyes are a dark off-amber yellow color.

Strengths and weaknesses

Sopapilla is pretty pathetic in battle, and chooses to flee rather than fly. She is an extremely fast and agile flier and could easily outfly any opponent. However, Sopapilla is afraid of flying, so she opts to walk and climb to get around. Sopapilla is very empathetic and likes giving random people hugs. She feels very insecure towards her fear of flying and towards her adopted parents. She is also able to grasp concepts pretty quickly.
